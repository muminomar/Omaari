Teaching Philosophy

My approach to teaching emphasizes hands-on learning and bridging theoretical concepts with practical implementation. In computer engineering courses, I focus on:

  • Connecting hardware concepts with software applications
  • Implementing projects that solve real-world problems
  • Encouraging collaborative learning through team projects
  • Adapting teaching methods to diverse learning styles
