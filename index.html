About Me

Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Engineering at KFUPM

Expected Graduation: November 2025

I am a computer engineering researcher specializing in federated learning, IoT security, and trusted systems. My Ph.D. research focuses on developing a "Multi-Objective-Trusted Federated Learning System For Heterogeneous IoT Environment" under the supervision of Dr. Muhamed F. Mudawar at KFUPM.

With a strong background in both hardware (computer architecture, scratchpad memory) and software (distributed systems, blockchain), I aim to bridge the gap between theoretical innovation and practical implementation in secure computing systems.
