About Me

Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Engineering at KFUPM

Expected Graduation: November 2025

I am a computer engineering researcher specializing in Federated Learning (FL) and AI applications for IoT systems. With a strong foundation in hardware design and computer architecture, I now focus on developing trusted distributed systems for resource-constrained environments.

My Ph.D. research at KFUPM centers on "Multi-Objective-Trusted Federated Learning System For Heterogeneous IoT Environment", where I design frameworks that balance:

  • Privacy preservation in distributed AI
  • Resource optimization for edge devices
  • Trust mechanisms for heterogeneous networks
  • Robustness against adversarial attacks
