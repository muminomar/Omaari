Research Focus

Ph.D. Dissertation

Multi-Objective-Trusted Federated Learning System For Heterogeneous IoT Environment

Developing a federated learning framework that ensures trust, privacy, and efficiency in heterogeneous IoT networks. The system addresses:

  • Security vulnerabilities in distributed learning
  • Resource constraints of IoT devices
  • Heterogeneity in network architectures
  • Multi-objective optimization (accuracy, latency, energy)
